Object-oriented programming (OOP)

is a style of programming characterized by the identification of classes of objects closely linked with the methods (functions) with which they are associated.

4 PILLAR IN OOP

encapsulation, abstraction, inheritance, and polymorphism. These four pillars provide the foundation for writing maintainable and scalable code.
